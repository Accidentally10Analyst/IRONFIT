Workout wear improves your form, prevents rashes, and keeps you comfortable during intense sessions.

  • Why it's important: Reduces sweat accumulation and enhances movement.
  • If you skip it: You risk irritation and discomfort leading to lower motivation.
  • Alternatives: Dry-fit T-shirts, yoga pants, anti-odor fabrics.

Supplements fill nutritional gaps and enhance recovery post workout.

  • Why it's important: Helps meet protein or vitamin needs for growth.
  • If you skip it: Your recovery slows down and fatigue increases.
  • Alternatives: Protein-rich diet, fortified foods, herbal tonics.

Get fit at home with the right gear: bands, dumbbells, mats, and more.

  • Why it's important: Helps perform focused exercises safely.
  • If you skip it: You rely on limited bodyweight exercises only.
  • Alternatives: Water bottles, stairs, resistance bands.

The little things that matter: bottles, straps, gloves, and more.

  • Why it's important: Supports better grip, hydration, comfort.
  • If you skip it: Leads to fatigue, poor form or injury risk.
  • Alternatives: DIY grips, reusable water bottles.
